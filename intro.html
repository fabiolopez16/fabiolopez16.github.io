Hi, my name is Fabio!

I was born in Havana, Cuba and I am currently 16 years old.
In my early years, for as long as I can remember, I was always drawing. Before moving to the U.S. I was always trying to find what I was best at.

Moving to the U.S. was hard. Not knowing English was the most difficult challenge to overcome. Thankfully, almost everybody knows a little bit of Spanish here in Miami. My favorite way to spend time to this day is drawing or doing oyher things I love like, playing my favorite videogames or writing poems.

I moved a lot before settling where I am now. I currently attend Miami Arts Studio at Zelda Glazer, a great school, and I am enrolled in the Tech Production magnet. In there I learned how to really be unique and creative with my work thanks to my magnet teacher Mr. Mathew Pate.

In the other hand my art teacher, Mr. Brian Reedy, really helped me find what I was best at and thanks to him, I am where I stand right now. He is always trying to really bring the best out of me. He always pushes me to try my best and I will always be thankful for everything he did for me!
